Practice 01

Online Brand

Supply Chain Intelligence & Digital Enforcement

Counterfeits surface on digital platforms faster than traditional monitoring can respond. Anti-piracy challenges compound this: unauthorized streaming, counterfeit software distribution, and digital content infringement run parallel to physical counterfeiting. Ecommerce platforms reveal supply chain networks—we map the networks, not just the listings. We coordinate surveillance, takedowns, and law enforcement where evidence warrants.

Continuous surveillance across ecommerce platforms, social commerce channels, and digital marketplaces.

  • Seller network & supply chain mapping
  • Anti-piracy detection & monitoring
  • Evidence documentation for action
02

Disrupt Networks

Converting intelligence into enforcement that breaks supply chains, not just removes listings.

  • Takedown coordination & execution
  • Seller network disruption
  • Law enforcement liaison & raids
  • Supply chain tracing & disruption
03

Protecting digital assets and staying ahead of emerging threats and recurrence patterns.

Practice 02

Customs
Enforcement

Intelligence Coordination & Border Enforcement

Millions of counterfeit shipments move through Asia Pacific's ports annually. Customs enforcement is typically reactive—goods arrive, customs inspects if flagged, enforcement happens if discovered. Proactive intelligence gets ahead of counterfeits before they reach customs.

01

Intelligence & Targeting

Proactive trade intelligence across major ports and border corridors across Asia Pacific.

  • Trade flow analysis & tracking
  • High-risk HS code identification
  • Counterfeit network & shipper mapping
  • Pre-arrival intelligence for targeting
02

Customs Infrastructure

Building legal and operational infrastructure that empowers customs to act on your behalf.

  • IPR recordation & customs registration
  • Product authentication documentation
  • Authorized importer verification
  • Port-level engagement & management
03

Enforcement Coordination

Translating intelligence and infrastructure into enforcement outcomes at scale.

  • Customs officer engagement
  • Law enforcement agency liaison
  • Intelligence sharing & support
  • Seizure follow-through & destruction

"Pre-arrival intelligence moves customs from reactive seizure to proactive interdiction."
